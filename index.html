Current Snapshot
Primary KPIs — LinkedIn (post-level · Post Performance)
Primary KPI definitions▸

Engagement Rate — engagements ÷ impressions on posts published in the period; how well content resonates relative to how many saw it.

Content Reach — total impressions on posts published in the period; how far content traveled.

Stickiness — Shares — times posts were reshared (amplification).

Stickiness — Comments — comments on posts (conversation).

Stickiness — Saves — times posts were saved; not a LinkedIn metric, so it reads 0.

Video Views — views of video posts; currently unreliable in Sprout (known data issue).

Secondary KPIs (profile-level · LinkedIn Pages)
Secondary KPI definitions▸

Profile Impressions — impressions on the Pindrop LinkedIn Page itself — brand interest beyond any single post.

Channel Growth — net new followers (gained minus lost) during the period.

Total Followers — cumulative LinkedIn Page follower count.

Traffic to Site — Organic Social + Organic Video (GA4)
Traffic KPI definitions▸

Sessions — visits to the site arriving from organic social and organic video sources.

Total / New Users — unique visitors, and the first-time subset, from those sources.

Engagement Rate (site) — share of sessions that were engaged (lasted 10s+, converted, or had 2+ pageviews).

Session Time — average session duration.

Organic Share — organic (social + video) sessions as a percent of all site sessions.

Organic Social + Organic Video — share of total sessions
